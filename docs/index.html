Clean Hydrogen Resilience Model (Electicity)

Open techno-economic model for green-hydrogen microgrids and heavy-duty FCEV, with LCOE/LCOH/TCO and a supply-chain resilience simulation. Two reference units: a Class 8 fuel-cell-electric truck under one million USD, and a two megawatt district hybrid microgrid under thirty-six million USD.
