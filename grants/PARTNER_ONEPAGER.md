# ELECTICITY - Consortium Partner Brief

> One-page recruitment brief for a Horizon Europe Cluster 5 (RIA) consortium.
> Send to prospective coordinators, universities, and industry partners.
> Numbers are scenario outputs from the open model, not forecasts. See [DISCLAIMER.md](../DISCLAIMER.md).

## The project in three lines

A water-medium green-hydrogen research program with two grounded tracks: a Class 8 FCEV
truck powertrain bench (under $1M, 24 months) and a 2 MW district hybrid microgrid
(under $36M, 36 months). The defensible asset is not a cell or a stack, it is the
**published, third-party-verifiable integration economics** at the smallest unit a
balance sheet can underwrite.

This brief recruits partners for the **bench track only**, shaped as a Horizon Europe
Cluster 5 Research and Innovation Action (RIA, TRL 4-6, 100% funded).

## Why now

- REPowerEU targets 10 Mt domestic renewable H2 by 2030; the frontier is system
  integration, not the device.
- The open cost model already prints honest numbers: district LCOH $1.90-$5.22/kg across
  three site archetypes; Class 8 FCEV TCO $0.91-$1.34/km at $4-$9/kg H2, stated straight
  against diesel ($0.78/km) and BEV ($0.71/km) references.
- We do not over-claim. BEV wins passenger duty cycles. The FCEV niche is heavy-duty
  long-haul where battery mass and refuel time flip the comparison. The bench measures
  exactly that boundary.

## What we are asking partners to do

Horizon collaborative RIA needs **at least 3 independent legal entities from 3 different
EU or associated states**. Open roles:

| Role | What you bring | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| Coordinator | Prior Horizon delivery, owns DMP/ethics/Open Science | Lead PI visibility, indirect-cost recovery, coordination budget |
| University / national lab | Independent metrology, measurement campaign, peer-review lead | Publications, instrumented dataset, 100% direct-cost funding |
| Industry partner | FCEV platform access or MW-class electrolyser with telemetry | Bench data on your hardware, RFNBO-compliance evidence |

Optional/later: EPC, host community, standards liaison (CEN/CENELEC, ISO TC 197),
ethics and safety advisor. These attach to the district track, not the bench RIA.
